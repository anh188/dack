Frequently asked questions when ordering flowers online

I. How to Order and Pay

When you order flowers at Dien Hoa Thu Do, you don't fully understand how to order, pay... and many other problems that arise. So, to help you better understand Thu Do Flower Dien, we would like to answer some of the following frequently asked questions:

1. How to order from Dien Hoa Thu Do?

You can order in the following ways:

+ Buy directly on website www.abc.com

– Clik to cart and fill out all information below

+ Order via Hotline (24/7)

+ Message via Zalo: 0123456789

2. How long is the best time to pre-order flowers before delivery?

In all cases, we encourage you to order flowers 24 hours in advance, this helps us be able to help you better. However, in some important unexpected cases and simple flower designs, we can still accept orders 1 to 2 hours in advance. We will try our best to help you

3. How will Dien Hoa confirm orders?

When you place an order successfully, you will receive an email notifying you of the detailed content of your successful order via the email you registered on the website. After receiving your order, we will call or email you to confirm your order
